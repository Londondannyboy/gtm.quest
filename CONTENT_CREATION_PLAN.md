- **20 B2B Marketing Agency pages** (country-level)

---

## Content Template Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- H1 with exact keyword + year
- Dynamic agency count and budget stats
- Country context tagline

### 2. Why Choose [Country] Section (~300 words)
- 3 paragraphs with market context
- 3+ external links to authoritative sources:
  - Wikipedia economy page
  - National statistics agency
  - Government/trade agency
  - World Bank data
  - EU/regional data

### 3. Services Section (~250 words)
- 4 service categories in 2x2 grid:
  - Demand Generation & Lead Gen
  - Account-Based Marketing (ABM)
  - Content Marketing & SEO
  - Market Entry Strategy
- Links to marketing platforms (HubSpot, Demandbase, etc.)

### 4. Industries Section (~300 words)
- 4 key industries for that market
- Industry-specific stats and context
- Links to industry data sources

### 5. How to Choose Section (~200 words)
- 3 selection criteria:
  - Industry Experience
  - Language & Cultural Capabilities
  - Technology Stack & Integration

### 6. Agency Directory
- Dynamic agency cards from database

### 7. FAQ Section (4 questions)
- With FAQPage schema markup
- Questions:
  1. Typical cost
  2. Why choose [country] agencies
  3. Industry specializations
  4. Timeline for results

### 8. Related Markets Section
- 6 related country links in grid
- Contextual descriptions

### 9. Resources Section
- 4 internal resource links:
  - /b2b-gtm-strategy
  - /planner
  - /articles
  - /about

### 10. CTA Section
- Strategy planner promotion

- [ ] 15+ internal links

---

## Link Validation Process

For each external link:
1. Check HTTP status (must be 200 or valid redirect)
2. Verify not paywalled
3. Verify not soft 404
4. Verify content is relevant and authoritative
5. Prefer .gov, .org, Wikipedia, major publications
